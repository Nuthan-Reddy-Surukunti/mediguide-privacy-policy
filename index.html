Privacy Policy for MediGuide AI

Last updated: November 9, 2025

1. INTRODUCTION

MediGuide AI ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

a) Personal Information:

  • Email address (for account authentication via Firebase)
  • Name and profile photo (optional, via Google Sign-In)
  • User preferences and settings

b) Location Data:

  • Approximate location (GPS coordinates) to detect your state/region
  • Used only to show relevant emergency contacts for your area
  • Collection is optional and can be denied

c) Usage Data:

  • App interactions and usage statistics
  • Search queries within the app
  • Features accessed
  • Collected via Firebase Analytics

d) Voice Data:

  • Voice commands for AI assistant (processed in real-time, not stored)
  • Sent to Google Gemini AI for processing
  • Not recorded or saved on our servers

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • Provide and improve app functionality
  • Authenticate your account (Firebase Auth)
  • Show emergency contacts relevant to your location
  • Provide AI-powered voice assistance
  • Analyze app usage to improve features
  • Send optional learning reminders

4. DATA SHARING

We do NOT sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Google Firebase (for authentication and analytics)
  • Google Gemini AI (for voice assistant processing)

5. DATA SECURITY

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Passwords are securely managed by Firebase
  • Location data is never stored permanently
  • Voice data is processed in real-time and not saved

6. YOUR RIGHTS

You can:

  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Deny location permissions (app still works with manual state selection)
  • Deny microphone permissions (voice assistant disabled)
  • Request data export or deletion by emailing us at the contact below.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

MediGuide AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Please review their privacy policies:
